HTML Guide: Nested Lists and Tables

Nested Lists

A nested list is a list placed inside another list. This is useful for creating a hierarchical structure for your content, such as an outline or a menu.

1. Example of a Nested List

  • Countries
    1. India
      • Delhi
      • Mumbai
      • Goa
    2. USA
    3. England
  • Continents
    1. Asia
    2. North America
    3. Europe
